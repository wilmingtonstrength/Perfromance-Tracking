Features

Everything You Need to Run a
Data Driven Program

From test day to parent meetings, Kaimetric keeps your athlete data organized, visible, and actionable.

Batch Test Entry

Enter results for your entire team in one session. Select a test, see every athlete, punch in the numbers. No more one by one data entry across multiple tabs.

Athlete Profiles with PR Tracking

Every athlete gets a profile with their full test history, personal records, and progress charts that update automatically as you log new data.

Record Board with TV Mode

A live, always current record board you can throw on the TV in your weight room. Splits by gender, age group, and weight class. No more dry erase markers.

Shareable Athlete Report Cards

Generate clean, branded progress reports you can share directly with parents. Show them exactly how their kid is improving with real numbers and visuals.

Results Explorer

Compare athletes across age groups, filter by test type, and surface your top performers instantly. The insights you need to train smarter and recruit better.

Jump Calculator and Auto Conversions

Built in jump power calculator and automatic max velocity conversions for fly tests. The small tools that save you from pulling out your phone mid session.
